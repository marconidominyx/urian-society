Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Urian Society?

Join our Discord server and follow the onboarding process. We'll guide you through the community guidelines and help you get started!

What events do you host?

We host gaming tournaments, creator workshops, community meetups, and various other events. Check our events page for upcoming activities!

Is there a membership fee?

No, joining the Urian Society is completely free! We believe in making our community accessible to everyone.

How can I contribute to the community?

You can participate in events, share your content, help other members, or even organize your own events with our support!
